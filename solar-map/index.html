Instructions

Click on a coordinate to get started and see more information.
The first chart plots the entire years solar data for the selected point at the selected hour
The second chart plots the entire selected days solar data for the selected point
Color of circle on the main svg represents the Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI) at that coordinate. White means low GHI, black means high GHI.
Move the week/hour sliders to see GHI over time! The below graphs will also move along time.
Click the switch to see Temperature over time! NOTE: coming soon!
See Jupyter Notebook for more information about GHI, Temperature values, and the overall dataset.
